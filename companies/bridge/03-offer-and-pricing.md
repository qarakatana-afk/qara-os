## Purpose of This Document

This document defines what Bridge offers and what it charges. Pricing must align with the human outcome and Qara OS values — not engineered to maximize extraction.

> **PROPOSAL NOTICE:** All pricing, tier structures, trial lengths, message limits, and subscription structures in this document are **proposals** — hypotheses that require founder review, validation research, and explicit founder approval before any pricing or tier structure is communicated to users or implemented. These figures are not the defined Bridge pricing model. Do not treat them as finalized decisions.

---

## 1. Core Offer

```
What the customer receives: Bridge is a communication companion that removes barriers 
for better connections and understanding — helping people communicate with those they 
care about across language, hearing, and other barriers that technology can responsibly 
remove.

What problem it solves for them: Communication barriers in personal relationships, 
appointments, and daily life — the feeling of being unable to say what you really mean, 
or to fully understand what is happening around you.

What they must do to use it: Download the app and begin. The specific setup varies 
by companion module — Bridge Translation asks for a connection (a name and language); 
Bridge Appointment asks for the appointment context. In all cases, the goal is that 
the person can begin in under 2 minutes.

What they do not get:
  - Bridge is not a general translation tool for tasks, documents, or professional work
  - Bridge is not a social network — there is no public profile, feed, or community
  - Bridge does not work as a certified interpreter in formal legal or regulatory contexts
```

## 3. Pricing

> [ASSUMPTION — PROPOSAL] All price points below are hypotheses for founder review. These specific prices ($9.99, $17.99, etc.), trial length (14 days), and billing structure have not been validated and must not be communicated to users or implemented without explicit founder approval. Willingness-to-pay research is required before pricing is finalized.

```
Proposed price (founder approval required before use):
  Personal: $9.99/month or $89/year (equivalent to $7.42/month)
  Family: $17.99/month or $159/year (equivalent to $13.25/month)
  Free tier: Free, with the limits described above

Billing model (proposed): Monthly or Annual subscription; annual at a discount; 
no auto-renewing without clear disclosure at purchase; reminder before annual renewal

Free trial (proposed): 14-day free trial of Personal tier for new accounts; 
no payment information required to start the trial

Pricing rationale:
  - Customer willingness to pay basis: [ASSUMPTION] $9.99/month is within the range 
    that consumers pay for communication-adjacent apps; willingness to pay requires 
    validation in pre-build user research
  - Competitive pricing benchmark: Duolingo Plus ~$7–13/month; DeepL Pro from $8.99/month; 
    general AI assistants $20/month [INFERENCE — subject to change; requires fresh research]
  - Unit economics implication: At $9.99/month, covering infrastructure and API costs 
    at low scale requires careful cost management — see 08-financial-plan.md

Price points that are prohibited by Qara OS values:
  - Must not charge in ways that exploit limited income or vulnerability
  - Must not use dark pricing patterns: No anchoring with fake "original prices"; 
    no hidden fees; no price increases at renewal without 30-day advance notice; 
    no paywalls that appear mid-conversation
```

## 4. Value Proposition

> [ASSUMPTION — PROPOSAL] The value proposition below is a draft for founder review. Claims about Bridge's competitive uniqueness ("the only companion...") are not supported by documented market research and must not be used in any external communication without validation.

Bridge is a communication companion that removes barriers so people can connect and be understood in the moments that matter most — and gets out of the way so they can focus on the person, not the technology.

## 5. Offer Constraints (Qara OS)

```
- Must not monetize user data: Confirmed. Bridge does not sell, license, or share 
  conversation content or relationship metadata for any commercial purpose.

- Must not create lock-in that removes user agency: Confirmed. Users can export all 
  their data at any time. Cancellation is simple and immediate. Conversation history 
  and relationship data are fully portable. No lock-in by proprietary format.

- Must not include engagement-maximizing mechanisms not aligned with human outcome: 
  Confirmed. No streak mechanics, no gamified message counts, no "you haven't called 
  [Name] in X days" prompts. Message limits on the free tier exist as a business 
  necessity, not an engagement driver.
```
