## 2. The Founding Belief

What is the core belief this company is built on? This is the insight that makes the company worth building.

- A genuine belief, not a market observation.
- Should be falsifiable: if the belief is wrong, the company thesis fails.

```
We believe: [State the founding belief]
If this belief is wrong: [What would that mean for the company]
Evidence that supports this belief: 
  [FACT] ...
  [INFERENCE] ...
  [ASSUMPTION] ...
```
