## Purpose of This Document

This document defines how the company plans to use resources and reach financial sustainability. Financial plans must be honest about uncertainty — label every number with its confidence level. A financial plan that requires harming users to be viable is not acceptable under Qara OS.

---

## 1. Financial Principles

```
1. Revenue model does not rely on selling user data or harvesting attention
2. Growth projections are labeled by confidence level, not presented as certainty
3. The plan accounts for operating under Qara OS constraints (slower growth, higher trust)
4. Sustainability is defined as covering costs with revenue — not requiring continuous investment
```
