## Purpose of This Document

This document defines the human outcome this product is designed to achieve with precision sufficient for testing and decision-making. Every product feature, design choice, and release decision must be traceable to this outcome.

---

## 1. Primary Human Outcome

State the single most important improvement this product creates for the person using it.

- Must be specific to this product's user, not a generic good.
- Must be falsifiable: you should be able to tell if it is or is not happening.

```
Primary outcome: [State it here]

Person affected: [Who specifically — be precise about life stage, context, or circumstance]

Observable signal that the outcome is occurring: [What would you see, hear, or measure?]

Observable signal that the outcome is NOT occurring: [What would indicate failure?]
```
