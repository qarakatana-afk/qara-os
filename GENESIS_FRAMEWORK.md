# Genesis Framework

Genesis is the product discovery engine for Qara Labs.

Its purpose is not to generate random ideas.

Its purpose is to discover which products naturally emerge from the company philosophy.

## Core Input

Genesis reads:

- Founder's Charter
- Art of Presence
- Bridge Bible
- Companion Constitution
- UX Principles
- Memory Philosophy
- Engineering Principles

## Core Output

For every product concept, Genesis returns:

- Purpose
- Human need
- Friction removed
- Presence strengthened
- Understanding deepened
- Trust earned
- Simplicity score
- Evidence needed
- Build recommendation

## The Genesis Question

> If these principles were the only thing we knew, what products would naturally deserve to exist?

## The Rule

Do not optimize first for market size.

Do not optimize first for technical impressiveness.

Optimize first for alignment.

Then test evidence.

## Product Lifecycle

Truth → Belief → Experiment → Evidence → Decision.

Products are experiments.

The philosophy is the source.
