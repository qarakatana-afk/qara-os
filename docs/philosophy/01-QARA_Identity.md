# QARA Identity

## Purpose
This specification defines the enduring identity of Qara Labs.

## Identity
Qara Labs exists to discover, develop, and steward technologies that reduce unnecessary friction in human experience.
Technology is a means, never an objective.
Products are temporary expressions of enduring principles.

## Definitions
Unnecessary Friction: Any obstacle that does not contribute to understanding, trust, safety, autonomy, or human capability.
Human Outcomes: Measurable improvements in a person's ability to understand, decide, connect, create, or act.

## Mission
Create technologies that strengthen understanding of self and others.

## Vision
Technology should quietly support human life rather than compete for human attention.

## Principles
- People precede products.
- Truth precedes strategy.
- Presence precedes performance.
- Trust is earned.
- Simplicity is respect.
- Stewardship exceeds ownership.

## Authority
This specification is subordinate only to the Founder's Charter.
Interpretations shall favor consistency with the Charter rather than individual preference.
Amendments shall follow the Charter.
