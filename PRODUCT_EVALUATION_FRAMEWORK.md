# Product Evaluation Framework

Every product idea must pass through these gates.

## Gate 1 — Truth

What is the human truth behind this idea?

Is this a real problem or an invented one?

## Gate 2 — Presence

Does this help someone become more present?

Or does it add more noise?

## Gate 3 — Understanding

Does this help people understand themselves, each other, or reality more clearly?

## Gate 4 — Trust

Does this earn trust?

Does it protect attention, privacy, and agency?

## Gate 5 — Simplicity

Can a first-time user understand what to do?

Can the product be simpler?

## Gate 6 — Evidence

What evidence would prove this deserves to continue?

What evidence would tell us to stop?

## Recommendation Levels

- Reject
- Explore
- Prototype
- MVP
- Build Immediately

## Final Question

If this product succeeds, how does someone’s life become meaningfully better?
