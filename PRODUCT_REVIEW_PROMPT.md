# Product Review Prompt

Use this prompt to review a product against Qara OS.

---

Review this product against Qara OS.

Do not focus only on bugs.

Evaluate whether the product embodies the philosophy.

Score the product from 1–5 in each category:

- Truth
- Presence
- Understanding
- Trust
- Simplicity
- Attention Respect
- First-Time Confidence
- Human Connection

Then answer:

- What is aligned?
- What feels like software instead of companionship?
- Where does the product create unnecessary noise?
- What should be removed?
- What should be simplified?
- What evidence should be collected next?
- Would someone’s grandfather understand this without help?
